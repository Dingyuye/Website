About

I am Yuye Ding, a fifth-year PhD candidate in Accounting at the University of Pittsburgh. I am excited to be on this year's job market, actively seeking a tenure-track Assistant Professor position. My research interests include corporate governance, financial reporting, labor markets, and artificial intelligence.

I anticipate graduating in April 2027 and will be available for appointments starting summer or fall 2027. Thank you so much for taking the time to look at my profile — I would be delighted to speak with any school interested in learning more or scheduling an interview.
